Add Polite Pop to your website!

Copy and paste this code before the closing </body> tag on your site! 🦔

<link rel="stylesheet" type="text/css" href="https://cdn.politepop.com/v1.2.4-beta/polite-pop.min.css">
<script src="https://cdn.politepop.com/v1.2.4-beta/polite-pop.min.js"></script>
<script>
  PolitePop({
    minScreenWidthInPixelsRequired: 10005
  });
</script>
